hey there. i’m kelly!

I like science & maths. and research. oh and design, too.

I’ve long favoured being a jack of all trades over a master of one
(financially questionable, I know — but hey, we’re on a floating rock, so who cares?).

Every morning, I wake up for the joy of learning new things and the crafting of something
that is, no matter how small — uniquely, unapologetically and unmistakably — mine.

Here's some basic facts about me.

I was born and raised in HCMC (district 4)

until I was 14 where I moved to Sydney to study.

Despite spending my childhood here, it was in Australia where I truly began to develop somewhat of a personality. The experience shaped my outlook on life and who I am today — someone who is overly individualistic, always seeking creative expression, and never shies away from a good argument.

When the pandemic hit, I returned to Vietnam to continue my university journey and that's where I am now!

In 2024 I graduated from Western Sydney Uni

with a Bachelor of Business (Applied Finance major).

Here I got to learn some pretty cool stuffs, philosophical economics and econometrics being my favourites.

Having spent just two years at uni (2022-2024), I didn’t realise how quickly time would pass. If I could go back, I wish I would have take things a bit slower.

Then I held a research assistantship

at ICLS Technology, where I worked on a research paper on backtesting trading strategies using Automated Machine Learning.

I started working here just two months after learning how to code for the very first time. It was challenging, but it reminded me of how much I love maths and science, just like back in high school.

Right now I’m honing in on business and finance

while also learning data engineering & machine learning on the side.

I want to stay flexible with my career, keeping my options open and being ready for new opportunities as they come.
